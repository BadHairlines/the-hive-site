Community Rules

We keep The Hive competitive and welcoming. Follow the guidelines below to avoid warnings or bans.

Open Support Ticket Server Info

Core Expectations

Respect & Conduct

  • No hate speech, harassment, or targeted abuse.
  • Keep comms clear during staff interactions.
  • Follow Discord moderation requests promptly.

Combat Integrity

  • No combat logging or alt-account scouting.
  • Report suspected cheating with video evidence.
  • Exploit abuse results in immediate bans.

Base & Raiding

  • Raids only during scheduled windows.
  • Do not block spawns or safe hubs.
  • One major base per faction.

Enforcement

Strike System

Minor offenses receive a warning or short suspension. Repeated behavior escalates penalties.

Evidence Review

Staff review clips, screenshots, and logs. Decisions are final once evidence is verified.

Appeals

Appeals are handled through Discord tickets within 7 days of a ban.

Quick Checklist

✅ Verify raid windows before striking bases.
✅ Keep factions under 8 players on a single raid.
✅ Record your gameplay for disputes.
✅ Respect staff calls in global chat.
